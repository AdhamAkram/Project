FAQ

Tazkarti ID is a unique identification card used to identify a fan registered in Tazkarti system. It allows access to any sports stadium for matches around Egypt. Besides the ticket, it acts as an entry pass and an access tool to stadiums.

Fans MUST apply for a Tazkarti ID to be able to book tickets and attend matches

It costs 100 Egyptian pounds to obtain Tazkarti ID. However, to get Tazkarti ID, you must purchase a ticket.

In order to be able to get your ticket you must first register and then buy a ticket, after that you can collect your ticket with your ticket from any authorized outlet. Visit our stores page for more information.

You can pick it up from any of our authorized outlets. Visit Our Stores page for more information.

In case of losing your Tazkarti ID card, you must go to one of our authorized outlets and request a reprint, along with payment for the cost of the extraction, by presenting a valid national ID card or a valid passport.

No, you do not have to register again; your account is still valid. You can use your Tazkarti ID number and password to sign in and book tickets, and you can use your Tazkarti ID card to attend matches you book tickets for.

You cannot register for a Tazkarti ID by yourself as you are underage. However, you can ask an adult from your family to register for a Tazkarti ID and after approving the request, he or she can add you as a dependent. After approving the dependent request, he or she can book tickets for you.

In case your request is rejected, you can update only information that you are required to update. However, after accepting your request, you cannot edit any of the personal information you have provided; you can edit only your account information, such as your password and language of communication.

Use your Tazkarti ID and password to sign in to your account, or register online to create an account, and then sign in. Search for the match you want and book the required tickets. Pay for the tickets you booked either by credit card or through Fawry network. Print your tickets at any of our authorized outlets.
